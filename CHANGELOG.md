## [0.2.0] - 2026-07-19

### Research status

- Residual-level statistical arbitrage and all quantum modules frozen as audited negative-results archives.
- Classical dynamics pipeline remains active: causal parameter estimation, directional coupling, integrator replay.

### Added

- Committed-tree verification tool (`engine.tools.verify_repository`).
- Isolated wheel smoke-test job in CI.
- Canonical instrument manifest and state-schema contracts.

## [0.1.0] - 2026-07-18

### Added

- Initial repository structure.
- Classical dynamics simulator and identity-aware coupling.
- Quantum experiment modules: Lindblad, reservoir, MPS, kernel, process tomography, trajectories.
- CLI entry point `auractl`.
