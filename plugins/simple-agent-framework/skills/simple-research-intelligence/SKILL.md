Use `research_seed_library` first, then `research_search_papers` or `research_doi_metadata`. Record DOI/URL, method, data scope, timing assumptions, comparator, limitations, and exact claim. Use `research_evidence_check` before writing a research claim.

Never treat a paper as profitability, production, causality, or promotion proof. Do not alter model thresholds, inspect market data, or run promotable research from literature review.
