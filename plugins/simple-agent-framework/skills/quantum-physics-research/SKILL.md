name: quantum-physics-research
description: Research quantum physics, quantum information, open systems, and quantum-inspired finance with explicit physical and financial validity boundaries. Use for Hamiltonians, Lindblad dynamics, quantum trajectories, tensor networks, quantum kernels, quantum reservoir computing, or claims connecting physics to finance.
---

Start with primary papers or authoritative reviews via `simple-research`. State system, Hilbert space, Hamiltonian/channel, approximation, observable, units, numerical method, convergence checks, and physical interpretation.

For finance links, separate analogy from mechanism. Require a classical matched baseline, fixed data protocol, compute budget, out-of-sample evaluation, and uncertainty. Frozen quantum modules are negative-result research: no quantum advantage, alpha, execution, or physical-market claim follows from simulation.
