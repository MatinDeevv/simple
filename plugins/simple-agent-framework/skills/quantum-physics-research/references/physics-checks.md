# Physics checks

- Density matrix: Hermitian, positive semidefinite, trace one.
- Channel: completely positive trace preserving when claimed.
- Lindblad: dimensions, rates, units, and integration convergence recorded.
- Tensor network: bond dimension/truncation error reported.
- Quantum algorithm: noise model, shots, seeds, compilation, and classical baseline recorded.
- Finance analogy: label as analogy unless measurable causal mechanism exists.
