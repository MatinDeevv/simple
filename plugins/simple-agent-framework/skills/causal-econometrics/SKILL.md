description: Design and audit causal econometric and event-study research for financial time series. Use for treatment timing, event studies, difference-in-differences, synthetic controls, IVs, Granger claims, or causal discovery.
---

Write estimand, unit, treatment, decision time, outcome horizon, confounders, identification assumptions, placebo tests, anticipation window, inference method, and sensitivity analysis before estimation.

Granger predictability is not causal effect. Association is not mechanism. Event timestamp must distinguish publication, market awareness, and assessment time. Financial nonstationarity requires chronological validation and regime diagnostics.
