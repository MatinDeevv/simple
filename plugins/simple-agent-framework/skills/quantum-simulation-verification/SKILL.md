---
name: quantum-simulation-verification
description: Verify numerical quantum simulations, open-system dynamics, and quantum-computing experiments. Use for density matrices, Lindblad solvers, trajectories, tensor networks, quantum circuits, noise models, or quantum advantage claims.
---

Check dimensions, basis/order, Hermiticity, trace, positivity, CPTP conditions where claimed, units, solver tolerances, time-step/bond-dimension/shots sweeps, seed reproducibility, and matched classical baseline.

Numerical agreement alone does not establish physical validity. Simulation advantage does not establish hardware advantage. Quantum result does not establish financial alpha.
